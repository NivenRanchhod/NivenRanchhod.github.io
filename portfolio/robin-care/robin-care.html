The experience of cancer can have the unintended consequence of withdrawal from support when people need it the most. That is why we’ll keep an eye out and check in from time to time to offer a helping hand.
 
Our Services
Our care advocates are friendly, knowledgeable, and have decades of cancer navigation experience. We are here to help support and empower you every step of the way. Here is a sample of our services:
 
24/7 access to care advocates who specialize in helping patients cope with cancer treatment and symptoms
 
 
Connecting you to other patients and survivors who have experience with your diagnosis or treatment
 
 
Finding you support groups or licensed therapists
 
 
Connecting you to additional resources customized to your unique care needs
 
 
Guidance on using your benefits effectively through treatment and beyond
 
 
Helping you navigate complicated insurance bills or denials
 
 
Supporting you through feelings of overwhelm and stress
 
 
Helping you customize your wellness plan
